ABOUT

coldRead is a smart, interactive must-have app made for actors by an actor.

Replace your reader!

The first app that recognizes cues and runs lines with you naturally, like a real reader. A game-changer.


Memorize, rehearse, and self-tape all by yourself!

Stop bothering your friends to read with you. Don’t spend money on time-limiting recording studios.

 

Save money. Be independent. Save time.

 

Memorize, rehearse, and self-tape all by yourself. No internet connection, no email address, and no annoying signing-up process required.

 

With coldRead available in your pocket 24/7 you’ll be always ready, well prepared, and confident.

 

 

BE YOUR BEST SELF!

Get coldRead for FREE today on the App Store.

 

coldRead runs on iPhones, iPads, and silicon (M-chip) Macs. coldRead Remote app is available for Apple Watch.

 

Languages supported: English (US, GB, CA, IN, AU), Spanish (US, ES, MX), Italian, Portuguese (BR), Russian, Turkish, Chinese (MN, CA), German, French, Korean, Japanese.

FEATURES

Easy to use!

Record each line of your script, and you’re all set.

Lines will be transcribed as you record them.

Acts as your reader!

When you say the very last word of your line (the cue), coldRead will “read” the following line back to you, just like a real reader would.


YOU ARE IN CONTROL!

Rehearse anywhere and anytime you want. Change the pitch, speed up or slow down. Be loud or be quiet. It will follow along.

BUILT-IN tELEPROMPTER!

Memorize your lines quickly and efficiently. Use the built-in teleprompter until you’re off-book.

Self-tape!

Put yourself on tape for that last-minute audition or before you get into the room with a casting director. Take as many takes as you need, and take as much time as you want.
