THE WORK




Overview
ScenePartner.ai is an app that helps actors run lines and record auditions without needing a scene partner in the room. Actors upload their script, assign roles, and practice with an AI voice reader. I joined as Design Lead to create the brand identity and design the product experience.

Brand and Identity
We wanted the brand to feel light, approachable, and a little fun to offset the stress that comes with auditions. Yorick became the face of the product. He’s a happy skull inspired by Shakespeare, but treated in a way that feels modern and simple, not theatrical or intimidating.


Personality
Yorick is the skull from Hamlet. While not full of personality in that scene, we decided it would be a perfect metaphor for actors who are rehearsing and auditioning by themselves. 

To add the playfulness we wanted, we decided to make his eyes come to life and a smile on his face to welcome users into the world of ScenePartner. 

I created a range of stylizations for Yorick to theme different season of the year and ensure there's plenty of collectable sticker opportunities (one of my favorite aspects of a new brand!)

Product Design
The product had to balance flexibility with clarity. Actors are often juggling a lot while preparing a self-tape, so the experience needed to feel quick and reliable. I designed a clean, distraction-free interface that put the script front and center. Key features included:



Script Editing: Users can upload and tweak scripts easily.

Voice Options: The AI reader can switch tones and voices to better match a scene.

Teleprompter Mode: Lets actors perform hands-free with smooth scrolling text.


Line Editing
Working with clunky scripts
Audition sides usually come as PDFs that are hard to edit or mark up. Actors need flexibility to prepare their role cleanly.


Design Response: I designed an in-app script editor where users can assign roles, cut or adjust lines, and keep everything organized in one place.

Teleprompter Mode
Balancing performance and script
During self-tapes, actors were struggle to see the lines on their device.  Especially if they were on a tablet or mobile device.


Design Response: So, we increased type size to make it clear at a glance their line and the following. We also added arrow buttons to navigate their script on the fly.





Outcome
We launched ScenePartner.ai with a clear identity and a functional product that helps actors practice and record self-tapes on their own. The brand feels approachable, the product is easy to use, and the features focus on solving the real pain points of audition prep.

We've grown our consumer base with a focus on word-of-mouth marketing, our product works so well people talk about it. Now, we're in the top drama programs in the country and adding more users everyday!
