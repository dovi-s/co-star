PETER: [excited, waving a cowboy hat] Yeehaw! Look at me, I’m Landman! I’m the king of oil and dirt and… uh, whatever else they do! 

LOIS: [rolling her eyes, folding laundry] Peter, please tell me this is just another one of your phases. Like the time you tried to open that underwater taco stand. 

PETER: [defensive, clutching the hat] Hey, that would’ve worked if I’d found waterproof lettuce! No, Lois, this is serious. Being a landman is all about making deals and looking cool. I’m like a Texas James Bond, but with a clipboard! 

STEWIE: [sarcastic, sipping juice] Oh yes, nothing screams "cool" like a man in a cowboy hat haggling over mineral rights in a swamp. Truly riveting. 

BRIAN: [smirking] Didn’t you fail negotiating with Meg over a bag of chips last week?

PETER: [sulking] That was different. She brought up my cholesterol, and I panicked! 

CHRIS: [confused, holding a map] Wait, so what exactly does a landman do? Are you like, digging for treasure?

PETER: [grinning, puffing his chest] Chris, I negotiate land leases for oil companies. I’m basically the middleman between rich people and poor people. 

MEG: [snapping, from the couch] So, you’re screwing over poor people? Great, another reason to hate you. 

PETER: [offended, pointing a finger] Hey! Don’t you sass me, young lady. I’m providing a vital service. Without guys like me, the world wouldn’t have gas... or those little plastic things on the end of shoelaces! 

STEWIE: [mocking, raising an eyebrow] Ah, the aglets. Truly the cornerstone of modern civilization. Bravo, Landman.

LOIS: [firmly, crossing her arms] Peter, I don’t want you getting involved in some shady business. You always find a way to make things worse. Remember the time you tried to sell Stewie to that traveling circus?

STEWIE: [calm but irritated] We’re still unpacking the trauma from that one, thank you. 

PETER: [waving her off] Relax, Lois. I’ve got this all figured out. I’m meeting Mr. Oil Guy McRich tonight to close a big deal. We’re talking barrels of cash, baby!

BRIAN: [skeptical, leaning in] And by "barrels of cash," you mean you’ll probably blow it all on beer and novelty belt buckles, right?

PETER: [grinning] You know me so well, Brian. 

[Quagmire suddenly bursts through the door, wearing a cowboy outfit.]

QUAGMIRE: [excited, adjusting his hat] Did someone say Landman? Giggity! I’ve been practicing my lasso skills all week. 

PETER: [nodding, impressed] Now that’s what I’m talking about! Lois, get ready. We’re about to strike it rich or die trying!

LOIS: [exasperated, throwing her hands up] Oh, for the love of—why can’t you just stick to watching TV like a normal person?! 

STEWIE: [snarky, under his breath] Because he’s Peter Griffin, mother. Chaos is his natural habitat.
