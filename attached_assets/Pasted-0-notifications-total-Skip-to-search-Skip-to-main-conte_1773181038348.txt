How does AI know when you’re done talking?

Its not as easy as you might think, people don’t speak at the same cadence and occasionally pause in the middle of their conversations. In all situations, having realtime conversations between a human and an AI its a balancing act between low latency, and AI interruptions.

The “right” way to solve the problem is to do exactly what humans do. Humans wait for a little delay in the conversation, and we use the context of the conversation to know when its our time to jump in.

The way to translate this to code is to 1) have a little pause after someone is done talking and 2) train a lightweight machine learning model to predict if the person is done talking
